# AI Bridge Privacy

AI Bridge runs locally on the user's computer.

- The local bridge listens only on `127.0.0.1`.
- The pairing token is stored in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\AIBridge\bridge-token`.
- AI Bridge does not include analytics, advertising, or telemetry.
- Roblox project commands and results remain on the local bridge unless the user
  connects an external AI client. That client's own privacy policy then applies.
- The raw local bridge port must never be exposed directly to the internet.
